About 4epuhaPost

Our Story

  • 2010: 4epuhaPost was founded as a small delivery service in Kyiv, Ukraine, focusing on local deliveries.
  • 2015: The company expanded to cover all of Ukraine, introducing express delivery services and package insurance.
  • 2018: 4epuhaPost launched international shipping services, connecting Ukraine with major cities in Europe.
  • 2020: Despite the challenges of the global pandemic, 4epuhaPost introduced contactless delivery options and grew its workforce by 30%.
  • 2022: The company became the market leader in delivery services in Eastern Europe, with hubs in Poland, Moldova, and Romania.
  • 2024: 4epuhaPost entered the fuel supply market and expanded its logistics network across the EU, becoming a household name for fast and reliable delivery.
